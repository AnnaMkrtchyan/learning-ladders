Meet the team

Learning Ladders was founded in 2011 by Swati due to her vision as a parent to foster an environment for childrenâ€™s all rounded development.
Understanding that the value of education to children is the foundation of opportunity, civil life and success she decided to start an institution where there is emphasis on uniqueness of each child, their sense of wonder and commitment to young minds. This is a program where children laugh, learn, explore and grow into well rounded individuals.

Swati

Swati holds a Masters in Business Administration degree from Babson College, MA with specialization in Entrepreneurship and Global Management. She has nineteen years of experience with leading corporations and has been a successful entrepreneur having run her own business consulting company. Swati worked with under privileged children for 5 years which exposed her to the importance of providing a stimulating and loving care to kids. She is multilingual and is First Aid and CPR certified.

Zenaida

Zenaida is a graduate from NJCU in Psychology. She has over a decade of experience working with children and is skilled in using established behavior modification and early intervention programs. When working with children she brings the science and maths with fun to the class to foster love of learning. Zenaida is First Aid CPR certified. She is bilingual in English and Spanish.

Cherry

Cherry has a Bachelors in Psychology and a Masters in Early Childhood education. She is skilled in designing multi disciplinary lesson plans and in drafting multiple assessment technique. Cherry brings with her two decades of experience in the field of Early Childhood and Elementary Education. She is First Aid and CPR certified.

Siobhan

Sara is a graduate from Rutgers in Psychology. She has over five years of experience working with children in different setting. Siobhan is First Aid CPR certified. She is bilingual in English and Italian.
