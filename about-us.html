It is our belief that all children deserve a quality care, education and should have equal access to all learning opportunities. Our school has a tradition in providing a safe and welcoming wide array of exciting activities in a challenging academic environment. We take pride to have continued focus on meeting the needs of our diverse student population and prepare them to become globally responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Our extensive menu of extracurricular activities, both academic and recreational is unsurpassed. Our entire team is committed to establishing a school environment that allows our students to flourish and to grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially. We work together toward the success of all the children providing them a basis for lifelong learning.

To help keep open communication families are informed about all the good things happening at our school through a monthly newsletter. It contains children’s work, the school calendar listing activities, events, classroom happenings and reminders from the school office. To keep you updated of class activities, weekly updates are emailed in addition to sending daily experience sheets. We encourage families to participate in school activities on special occasions as well as volunteer in child’s classroom as schedule allows. Together with our families we form a strong partnership to ensure that all children receive an exceptional education.

Thank you for exploring our website and I welcome you to visit our school – allow us to take you on a journey to discover us in action.

School Director

Mission and Philosophy

The mission of our school is to foster independence, promote analytical thinking and creative problem solving by utilizing culturally and developmentally appropriate practices to provide stimulating early care experiences, which promote children’s social/emotional, physical, and cognitive development. We support children by laying strong foundations in being responsible, gracious and confident citizens of tomorrow’s world. Learning Ladders is committed to forming a partnership between parents and our school to provide a robust academic and social learning environment while adhering to the philosophy that learning is not a destination, but a journey.

At a glance

Learning Ladders is an independent, private, non secretarian International Baccalaureate PYP school located in the historic Paulus Hook in Jersey City. Our newest third campus is in downtown Grove St by the Path station.
Our diverse student body is comprised of families across all ethnicity, race and religion. Our school abides by all state law and regulations governing early childhood educational centers.

Learning Ladders is a day school for infants through Kindergarten with plans for further expansion.

Our team

Learning Ladders was founded in 2011 by Swati due to her vision as a parent to foster an environment for children’s all rounded development. Understanding that the value of education to children is the foundation of opportunity, civil life and success she decided to start an institution where there is emphasis on uniqueness of each child, their sense of wonder and commitment to young minds. This is a program where children laugh, learn, explore and grow into well rounded individuals.
