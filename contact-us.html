Contact Us

Learning Ladders has grown over the years to have three campuses. Our first and second campus are located in historic Paulus Hook of Jersey City. We are opposite to Colgate Park and are surrounded by trees along the Hudson river. Our children routinely go for a stroll on board walk and by Morris canal park.

This school is situated by the Essex street light rail station, just one block from the downtown ferry and is two blocks to Exchange place path station. We are on your way to work, shopping and weekend activities.

Our third campus is located in downtown Jersey City by Grove St Path station and 3 blocks from Harborside Light Rail station.
