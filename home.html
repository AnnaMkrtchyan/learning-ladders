Welcome to learning Ladders

We make learning interesting through creativity

It is our belief that all children deserve a quality care, education and should have equal access to all learning opportunities. Our school has a tradition in providing a safe and welcoming wide array of exciting activities in a challenging academic environment.

Our Mission

The mission of our school is to foster independence, promote analytical thinking and creative problem solving by utilizing culturally and developmentally appropriate practices to provide stimulating early care experiences, which promote children’s social/emotional, physical, and cognitive development. We support children by laying strong foundations in being responsible, gracious and confident citizens of tomorrow’s world.

About Us

Learning Ladders is an independent, private, non secretarian International Baccalaureate PYP school located in the historic Paulus Hook in Jersey City. Our newest third campus is in downtown Grove St by the Path station.
